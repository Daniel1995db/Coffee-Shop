AMENITIES

Bakery


Hate to be the bearer of bad news, but if you thought we only specialized in coffee, you're sadly mistaken. We love complementing your coffee with some baked goods.

Wifi and Music


Need to work? Need to save data? We've got you covered.

It's 2018. Why wouldn't we have Wifi? Just grab a cup, your laptop, and get to work. We'll even play those coffee shops, meditation, deep focus tunes you love.

Work Stations


We understand how much your work means to you. So we've taken the liberty to set up some work stations equipped with charging docs to keep you moving. We've even made it easy to collaborate with others. So briing your team members and friends.

Coffee Breaks & Dates


Meeting a "friend" are you? We get it. We've been there. Thats why we made comfortable seating for two. So all you need to do is sit back, relax, and impress.

Live Music Artists


We love live music. Come and listen to some local artist. Who new your neighbor was so talented?


Every Friday Night
